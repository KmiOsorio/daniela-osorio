ABOUT

I am an artist and designer

I work on the identities of my projects seeking outcomes that stem from processes and explorations rooted in the analog and the material, combined with digital experimentation.

My practice crosses editorial design, illustration, branding, museography, graphic work, ceramics, and collaborative processes.

I work with cultural institutions, independent brands, and projects that value time, experimentation, expression, and making.
